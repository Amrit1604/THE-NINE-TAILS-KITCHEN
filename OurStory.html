who are we?

ICONIC ANIME FOODS

We are The Nine Tails Kitchen, a unique Japanese restaurant that blends the rich culinary traditions of Japan with the captivating world of anime. Located in the heart of Kyoto, we create unforgettable dining experiences that transport you to a magical realm where every dish tells a story. Join us to savor the flavors of Japan while immersing yourself in the enchanting spirit of anime.

OUR MISSION

ICONIC ANIME FOODS

At The Nine Tails Kitchen, our mission is to celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Japan while bringing the imaginative world of anime to life. We are dedicated to crafting exceptional dining experiences that combine traditional flavors with creative storytelling. By sourcing the freshest ingredients and infusing our dishes with the magic of anime, we aim to delight our guests and transport them to a world where every meal is an adventure.
